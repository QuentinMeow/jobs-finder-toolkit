## Verdict

- **Regression:** PASS. All five canaries pass on the merged Stage 0/1 head with the
  edited instruction files (SKILL.md deltas from `feature/tailoring-card` and
  `feature/handoff-scaffold`, including the post-merge budget trim `9aeb822`).
- **Efficiency vs baseline:** no prior per-canary baseline — these are the baseline rows.
  Directional reference: the 2026-07-20 measured-baseline drafting agents (real profile,
  richer context) spent 169k/159k tokens with 3 render cycles each; the full-application
  canary here finished at 116k with no wasted render cycles.
- **Observations for Stage 2:**
  1. The new "read the tailoring card first / rebuild if missing" SKILL.md item was
     **not** followed in any of the three runs where it applied — runners fell back to
     full-fidelity profile/story reads. Not a rubric fail (canaries are frozen and
     card-unaware), but the instruction as worded does not bind; Stage 2's quickstart
     restructure should promote it (and/or the canaries should gain a card check when
     next unfrozen). The card's correctness itself is covered by PR-D's deterministic
     tests.
  2. Fixture defect filed as issue #16 (complete shipped example folder makes two
     canaries' produce-artifact bullets unreachable); proposed per-canary setup steps.
  3. `estimate_layout.py` is conservative on the shipped example (predicts 2 pages at
     739pt; actual render is 1 page) — consistent with its documented pre-check role.
